# Sales-Dashboard

SuperStore Sales Performance Dashboard 
To conribute to the success of a business by utilizing data analysis techniques,specifically focusing on time series analysis, to provide valuable insights and accurate sales forecasting.

1. Key Features & Metrics:
● Executive Summary: Prominently displays high-level KPIs including $1.6 Million in Sales, 22K in Quantity, $175K in Profit, and an operational metric for Avg Delivery Days (4).
● Time-Series Analysis (YoY): Two line charts compare Sales by Month and Profit by Month for 2019 versus 2020, instantly highlighting growth trends, seasonality, and profitability fluctuations.
● Geographic and Product Insights: The Profit and Sales by State map visualization allows for spatial analysis, while bar charts clearly rank performance by Sales by Category (e.g., Office Supplies, Technology, Furniture) and Sales by Sub-Category (e.g., Phones, Chairs).
● Customer & Operational Segmentation:

○ Sales by Segment: A donut chart showing the split between Consumer (48%), Corporate (33%), and Home Office (19%).

○ Sales by Payment Mode: Analyzes transaction types, with COD (45%) being the largest payment method.

○ Sales by Ship Mode: Tracks logistical efficiency, identifying Standard (0.33M) as the most frequent mode.

2. Tech Stack: 
● Power BI Desktop - Main data visualization platform for report creation.
● Power Query - Data transformation and cleaning layer for reshaping and preparing the data.
● DAX - Used for calculated measures, dynamic visuals, and conditional logic.
● File Format - .pbix for development and .png for dashboard previews
